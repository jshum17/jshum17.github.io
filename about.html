About Me

Hello There!

My name is Justin and I’m a 17-year-old studying at J.N Burnett Secondary in Richmond, BC.

I fell in love with coding when I built my first website in grade 9. Since then, I have taught myself to write various programming languages including HTML, Css & Javascript. I love to solve problems and challenge myself to come up with unique solutions. The satisfaction that comes with discovering just the right solution is what draws me into programming. In the future, my goal is to develop a proficiency in programming and I aspire to utilize my understanding in computer science to develop innovative technologies that will improve the lives of others.
